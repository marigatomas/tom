Biryani Rice Recipe

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken or Mutton or Vegetables
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Yogurt
  • Spices (Biryani masala, turmeric, chili powder, etc.)
  • Mint leaves
  • Coriander leaves
  • Oil or Ghee
  • Saffron (optional)
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Soak the rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Fry the onions until golden brown.
  3. Saut√© the ginger-garlic paste.
  4. Add the meat or vegetables and cook.
  5. Add the spices and yogurt.
  6. Layer the rice and the meat mixture in a pot.
  7. Add mint and coriander leaves.
  8. Cook on low heat until done.
  9. Garnish with saffron (optional).
