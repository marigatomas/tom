Spaghetti with Chicken Milanese Recipe

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Chicken breast
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Egg
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Carrot
  • Celery
  • White wine (optional)
  • Chicken broth
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Fresh basil
  • Butter

Instructions

  • Prepare the Chicken Milanese:
  • Pound the chicken breast to an even thickness.
  • Beat the egg in a shallow dish.
  • Mix breadcrumbs and Parmesan cheese in another dish.
  • Dip the chicken in the egg, then coat it with the breadcrumb mixture.
  • Fry the chicken in olive oil until golden brown and cooked through.
  • Set aside, keeping warm.
  • Prepare the Sauce:
  • Finely chop onion, garlic, carrot, and celery (soffritto).
  • Saut√© the soffritto in olive oil and butter until softened.
  • Add white wine (if using) and let it evaporate.
  • Add tomato sauce and chicken broth.
  • Simmer for at least 30 minutes, or until the sauce thickens. Season with salt and pepper.
  • Cook the Spaghetti:
  • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  • Add the spaghetti and cook according to package directions (al dente).
  • Drain the spaghetti, reserving some pasta water.
  • Combine and Serve:
  • Add the cooked spaghetti to the sauce. Toss to coat, adding pasta water if needed to adjust consistency.
  • Serve the spaghetti with the Chicken Milanese on top.
  • Garnish with fresh basil and additional Parmesan cheese, if desired.
